Welcome to DotaSight!

1. Once a dota game starts loading, proceed to step 2.
2. Start by clicking on "Enter Your Game Info on the right top of your screen
3. Follow the instructions (find the dota server_log.txt, and send us the contents through the form).
4. After a delay of a few seconds, the popup will dissapear and player specific data will load.
5. If the user does not have a dotabuff profile, there will be no player specific stats.
6. Select a hero on the right, and click on the player whom chose the hero.
7. Profit!
Caveats:
1. Hero default win rate is for 6.84 game-mode neutral data updated as of sunday
2. Player specific data is for ALL PICK in the PAST YEAR
3. Advantage calculation is in beta stage.
